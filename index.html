portfolio

Since 2021, I have had the privilege of serving as the photographer for the Arizona State Museum, building upon my extensive freelance photography experience in the five years prior to starting at ASM. My portfolio encompasses a diverse range of photography styles, including portrait, event, studio, and documentary photography, with a strong focus on anthropology, geography, folklore, and historical narratives.

I am passionate about creating documentary images that capture the essence of human experience and cultural heritage. As I continue to develop my craft, I am eager to expand my work further into the documentary field, where I can contribute to storytelling through visual media and celebrate the richness of our shared history.
